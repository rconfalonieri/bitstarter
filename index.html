Tired of the experience that current Tourism portals offer?

Being a travel agency or an end-user you can engage in browsing together the content of a Web application with the aim to take a collective decision and making an online shared experience.

Our platform WeBrowse is an innovative way to allow travel agencies and end-users to make an online shared experience such as the curation of travel packages, the selection of activities or the planning of a trip.

Are you a travel agency?

Through WeBrowse you will be able to modernise your tourism portal to:

  • Create travel packages in a collaborative way
  • Describe travel activities
  • Promote activities to end-users
